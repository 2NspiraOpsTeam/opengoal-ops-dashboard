# Email KPI Scorecard — Open Goal Soccer

Last Updated: 2026-03-03
Owner: Adam

## Weekly Metrics
1. Bounce Rate
2. Complaint/Spam Flag Rate
3. Open Rate
4. Reply Rate
5. Positive Reply Rate
6. Qualified Opportunity Rate
7. Avg Time to First Response

## Suggested Targets (Initial Baseline Phase)
- Time to first response: <= 4 business hours (all), <= 2h (high fit)
- Reply rate: improving trend week-over-week
- Positive + qualified rates: stable or improving by segment

## Weekly Review Template
- What improved?
- What degraded?
- Biggest bottleneck?
- Top 2 experiments for next week?
- Owner + due date for each change
