# Open Goal Soccer — Email Follow-up SOP + SLA (v1)

## Purpose
Standardize how the team monitors, responds to, and escalates inbound email replies from vetted outreach contacts to maximize qualified conversations and booked meetings.

## Scope
Applies to all inbound/outbound communication associated with Open Goal Soccer school/contact outreach campaigns.

## Team Roles (recommended operating split)
- **Maya (Comms Lead):** Inbox monitoring, triage, first-response drafting/sending, template management.
- **Brian (Vetting Lead):** Qualification scoring, fit/no-fit decisions, routing high-potential opportunities.
- **Adam (Research Lead):** Contact enrichment, missing-data resolution, additional target discovery.
- **Jeffrey (Exec Sponsor):** Final approvals for strategic escalations and priority opportunities.

---

## 1) SOP — Daily Operating Workflow

### A. Inbox Monitoring Cadence
- **Sweep 1:** 9:00 AM ET
- **Sweep 2:** 1:00 PM ET
- **Sweep 3:** 4:30 PM ET
- Optional late sweep at 7:00 PM ET during active campaign pushes.

### B. Labeling Taxonomy (Gmail)
Apply exactly one primary status label and optional support labels.

**Primary status labels**
- `OGS/New Lead`
- `OGS/Interested`
- `OGS/Needs Info`
- `OGS/Meeting Request`
- `OGS/Not Fit`
- `OGS/Closed Won`
- `OGS/Closed Lost`

**Support labels**
- `OGS/Priority-24h`
- `OGS/Waiting-On-Internal`
- `OGS/Waiting-On-Prospect`
- `OGS/Escalation`

### C. Triage Rules (Maya)
For each new inbound email:
1. Classify intent (interest, objection, referral, no-fit, logistics, other).
2. Apply status + support labels.
3. Log/update contact status in repository tracker (CSV/dashboard input).
4. Route to Brian if qualification is needed.
5. Respond using approved template variant (or custom response if needed).

### D. Qualification Rules (Brian)
Assess inbound leads against baseline fit criteria:
- School/organization relevance to youth soccer program goals
- Contact role influence (decision-maker vs influencer)
- Geographic or logistical fit
- Program readiness/timing

Decision outcomes:
- **Qualified:** mark `OGS/Interested` or `OGS/Meeting Request`
- **Needs info:** mark `OGS/Needs Info` with required data fields
- **Disqualify:** mark `OGS/Not Fit` with explicit reason code

### E. Research Enrichment (Adam)
Triggered when:
- Missing key contact data (title, direct line, correct inbox)
- Referral to additional stakeholders
- Need for deeper school/org context

Deliverables:
- Updated contact record
- New recommended contacts (if found)
- Notes for future messaging personalization

### F. Escalation Path
Escalate to Jeffrey when:
- High-value district/school opportunity
- Contract/partnership-level questions
- Any reply requiring executive commitment

Escalation method:
- Add `OGS/Escalation` label
- Post summary in team channel with: account, ask, urgency, recommended response

### G. End-of-Day (EOD) Summary (Owner: Maya)
Daily by 5:30 PM ET post in team channel:
- Total inbound replies
- # Interested
- # Meeting requests
- # Needs info
- # Disqualified (+ top reasons)
- Open items requiring team action next day

## 2) SLA — Service Levels

## A. First Response SLA
- **Business hours (8:30 AM–6:00 PM ET):** first response within **4 business hours**
- **Priority-24h tagged:** first response within **2 business hours**
- **After-hours inbound:** first response by **10:00 AM ET next business day**

## B. Internal Handoff SLA
- Maya → Brian (qualification review): within **2 business hours** of triage
- Brian → Maya (fit decision + direction): within **4 business hours**
- Brian/Maya → Adam (enrichment request): same sweep, no later than **EOD**
- Adam → Team (enrichment return): within **1 business day**

## C. Meeting Request SLA
- Acknowledge meeting interest within **2 business hours**
- Provide scheduling options within **1 business day**

## D. Escalation SLA
- Mark and escalate strategic items to Jeffrey within **1 business hour** of identification
- Executive reply guidance target: **same business day** (or next business day if after hours)

## E. Data Hygiene SLA
- CRM/repo tracker updates completed **same day** as inbound interaction
- No unresolved unlabeled inbound emails at day close

## 3) KPI Dashboard (Weekly)
Track every Friday:
- Reply rate
- Positive interest rate
- Meetings booked
- Qualification pass rate
- Disqualification reasons (top 5)
- Time-to-first-response (median)
- SLA compliance % by owner
